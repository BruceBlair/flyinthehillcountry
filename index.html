Ecosystem Branches

coolerifyoudid — Materials That Cool the World

The materials branch: highly reflective coatings, phase‑change composites, ground‑coupled thermal batteries, and passive cooling benches. Low‑cost, bio‑sourced, off‑grid heat mitigation for real people in real heat.

Highly Reflective — Civic Awareness & Collective Action

The civic branch: public installations, heat‑relief benches, and tools that help communities understand AGW heat buildup and the cooling potential of reflective surfaces. Turning technical cooling into shared civic momentum.

Snow White Reflections — Data, Sensing & Insight

The sensing branch: weather stations, thermal cameras, and ground‑truth nodes mapping the Hill Country’s heat in real time. A live dashboard showing what’s warming, what’s cooling, and where interventions matter most.

Self‑Awear — Cultural Signals

The cultural branch: wearables, prints, and reflective artifacts that carry the movement into everyday life. Not swag—signal. Small visible commitments to a cooler, more climate‑literate future.

Hilltop Healing Center — Land & Self Regeneration

The land‑healing branch: cedar management, water‑catchment literacy, and practical ecological stewardship. Heal the land → the land heals you → you heal the land. Ikigai rooted in soil.
