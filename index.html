Snow White Reflections — Data, Sensing & Insight

The sensing branch. A growing network of ground‑truth nodes that map the Hill Country's heat in real time and provide live video footage to finally make sense of turbulant unpredictable terrain. This is the intelligence layer: the data that allows scientists to analyze historical and live weather patterns describing the most underserved most interesting terrains in Texas and the United States. No longer do you need to rely on upper atmospheric radar, or weather stations 30 miles away to guess how the weather is in the Texas Hill Country. Serving clients that need this real time data, from hang glider pilots to emergency weather warning systems, if you need to know what is actually happening on the ground in the Texas Hill Country or for your private landing zone, Snow White Reflections is THE definitive source for ground aware weather patterns. We turn the historical and live grounded data feeds into a pool rich for any scientist, pilot or curious person that wants to know what the weather is like in their own lot. Reliably feeding projects that can use the live data to inform actionable decisions to fly or not to fly. Make decisions based on advanced ground aware sensors for information starved warning systems monitoring for extreme wind, fire and rain events that have always been an invisible hazard to the Texas Hill Country.
