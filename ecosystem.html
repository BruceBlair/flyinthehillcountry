Ecosystem Branches

coolerifyoudid — Materials That Cool the World

The materials branch. We design and deploy radiative‑cooling surfaces, phase‑change systems, and low‑cost thermal infrastructure that anyone can build with. From reflective coatings to ground‑coupled benches, coolerifyoudid is the hands‑on layer of the movement — the part you can touch, mix, paint, and install.

Highly Reflective — Civic Awareness & Collective Action

The civic branch. Highly Reflective brings cooling into public life through installations, heat‑relief benches, and community‑scale demonstrations. We’re building tools that help cities and citizens understand AGW heat buildup, how much PDRC is needed to counter it, UHI‑mitigation potential, and the big‑picture progress of the cooling movement.

Snow White Reflections — Data, Sensing & Insight

The sensing branch. A growing network of weather stations, thermal cameras, and ground‑truth nodes that map the Hill Country’s heat in real time. This is the intelligence layer: the data that shows what’s working, what’s warming, and where cooling interventions matter most.

Self‑Awear — Cultural Signals

The cultural branch. Wearables, prints, and reflective artifacts that carry the movement into everyday life. It’s not swag — it’s signal. A way for people to reflect the mission back into the world.

Hilltop Healing Center — Land & Self Regeneration

The human‑and‑land branch. Hilltop Healing Center teaches people how to read a landscape, manage cedar, slow and sink water, and guide natural systems back into balance. Small daily actions shift entire systems: heal the land → the land heals you → you heal the land. Ikigai, rooted in soil.
